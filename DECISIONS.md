
## Budget Structure 
Decision1: Budget  Per category
Reason: Enables meaningful alerts and analytics
Decision2: One transaction per category
Reason: To avoid MVP constraints
Decision3: Soft delete category
Reason: to hide transactions under a category and keep links intact
Decision4: Refresh + short-lived tokens
Reason: Avoid getting users logged out frequently.
Decision5: Read-only offline
Reason: offline write will be added in a later phase.
